A tablespoon of discovery


Our mission

To take people on a traveling journey one meal at a time, by offering a variety of accessible traditional recipes from all around the world.


Our goal

We've designed our website specifically to open the world's gastronomic doors to food lovers. Our aim is to display as many recipes as possible from all corners of the globe, so that you can discover new flavors, new cuisines and simply immerse yourself in new cultures! Whether you're looking for an original quick meal for an evening with friends, a top-of-the-range meal for a romantic dinner or a simple family meal on a weekday, our cuisine atlas have the right recipes for you.


What sets us apart

We know that cooking international dishes can sometimes be a challenge. That's why we offer clear and simple cooking instructions for each recipe, as well as several substitutes for the original ingredients that may not be available in your area. With our Cuisine Atlas, cooking a delicious Indian, Brazilian or Greek recipe from home, with or without all the original ingredients, is possible. Our website also offer advanced search options, so you can find the type of cuisine you want very quickly.


Contribute!

Want to contribute? If you'd like to share one of your great recipes with the world, share it with us here. We'll receive your recipe, and if it seems complete after review, we'll gladly publish it.
